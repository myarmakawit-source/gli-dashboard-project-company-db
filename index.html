Notes & Professional Observations

Data Definitions — Billing · Contract · Revenue (GL)

  • Revenue — GL (General Ledger): account balances by project × year 2018–2025 — includes non-billing items such as HO, internal units (DS, ME, HR, ST), adjustments Z_xxxxx and negatives (reversals) · total 4,258,603,041.01 THB
  • Billing — Invoice (Issue Invoice actual / AR): the «amount before VAT» from the invoice-issuance report by project × year 2015–2026 (incl. 1 undated invoice of GLI22010) · total 3,769,538,864.93 THB
  • Contract Value (Contract Amount): contract/PO value by project from the Contract Amt. column in report AR 4.3.1 (82 projects) · total 5,023,917,577.36 THB — compared with Billing it shows billing progress (Billing ÷ Contract)
  • Variance = Revenue (GL) − Billing = +489,064,176.08 THB · by-project status: matched to the baht 28 · variance 43 · GL only 26 · Invoice only 11

Considerations

  • Different lenses: the variance reflects the gap between «accounting revenue recognition (GL)» and «invoice issuance (AR)», plus scope/timing differences — review the definitions before drawing conclusions
  • Some codes don’t map 1:1: the GL holds GLI25003 / 25008 / 25009 combined, while Invoice splits them #1 / #2 — this group is not a 1:1 match
  • File Book5: attachment not yet received — once sent (Excel/CSV preferred) it will be reconciled against the GL for an extra layer of verification
  • Current GL source: built from the numeric table provided by the user, reconciling to 4,258,603,041.01 — matching the user’s computed total to the baht
